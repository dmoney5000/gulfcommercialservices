Cabinet & Carpentry FAQs

How much do custom cabinets cost?

The cost of custom cabinets depends on the size of the project, the materials, and whether you choose new cabinets or refacing. We provide a free, detailed quote before any work begins — call 727-314-1255.

Yes. We install new custom cabinets and built-ins, and we also offer cabinet refacing as a more affordable way to refresh your kitchen without a full replacement.
