Local Remodelers

Trusted remodeling for St. Petersburg homes

St. Pete's housing is full of character, from the 1920s craftsman bungalows of Historic Kenwood and the brick streets of the Old Northeast to the Mediterranean Revival homes on Snell Isle and modern condos downtown. Each of these calls for a different approach, and we tailor every tile layout, floor, and cabinet run to the home in front of us.

As your neighbors here in St. Petersburg, we know the local housing stock and the local climate. We steer clients toward materials that hold up in the heat and humidity — porcelain tile and luxury vinyl plank that resist moisture — and we handle permitting through the City of St. Petersburg and Pinellas County so you don't have to.

Questions

St. Petersburg Remodeling FAQs

Do you handle permits for remodeling projects in St. Petersburg?

Yes. For work that requires it, we pull the necessary permits through the City of St. Petersburg or Pinellas County and coordinate inspections, so you don't have to navigate the process yourself.

Can you remodel historic homes in Old Northeast or Kenwood?

Absolutely. Many homes in the Old Northeast and Historic Kenwood have non-standard dimensions and original detailing, and custom cabinetry, built-ins, and careful tile work are exactly where our team excels.

What flooring holds up best in St. Pete's humidity?

Porcelain tile and quality luxury vinyl plank are both excellent for our climate because they resist moisture and temperature swings. Engineered hardwood can also work well with the right installation.

Are you licensed and insured in St. Petersburg?

Yes, we are fully licensed and insured to perform remodeling work throughout St. Petersburg and Pinellas County, and we can provide documentation before your project starts.

Do you offer free quotes for St. Petersburg homeowners?

We do. Contact us at 727-314-1255 or through our quote form and we'll schedule a visit to assess your project and provide a free, no-obligation estimate.
