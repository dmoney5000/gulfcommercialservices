Local Remodelers

Trusted remodeling for Clearwater homes

Clearwater living means a lot of waterfront homes, beach condos, and properties on Island Estates and Sand Key where salt air and moisture are constant factors. We pick tile, flooring, and finishes that stand up to that environment, and we're used to working within the access rules and HOA requirements common to beach and condo communities.

For coastal Clearwater properties we lean heavily on porcelain tile and waterproof luxury vinyl plank, and we pay close attention to moisture barriers and ventilation. Our cabinetry work uses materials and hardware chosen to resist the humidity that comes with living near the Gulf.

Do you work in beachfront condos and HOA communities in Clearwater?

Yes. We regularly work in condos and HOA-governed communities around Clearwater Beach, Island Estates, and Sand Key, and we coordinate with building management on access, elevator scheduling, and work-hour rules.

What flooring is best for a Clearwater beach property?

Waterproof luxury vinyl plank and porcelain tile are our top recommendations for coastal homes and condos because they handle sand, moisture, and salt air far better than traditional hardwood.

Can you remodel a bathroom or kitchen in a waterfront home?

Definitely. Tile and cabinetry are our specialties, and we take extra care with waterproofing and moisture protection on waterfront Clearwater properties.
