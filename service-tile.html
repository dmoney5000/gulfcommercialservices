Questions

Tile Installation FAQs

How much does tile installation cost in St. Petersburg?

Tile installation cost depends on the material you choose, the size of the area, and prep work like waterproofing or floor leveling. We provide a free, detailed estimate so you know the full price before any work begins — call 727-314-1255.

Do you install shower tile and kitchen backsplashes?

Yes. Shower tile, bathroom walls and floors, and kitchen backsplashes are among our most requested tile projects. We handle waterproofing, layout, and finishing for a durable, watertight result.

What tile materials do you work with?

We install ceramic, porcelain, marble, travertine, and natural stone, including large-format tile and mosaics. We'll help you pick a material that suits your style and holds up in Florida's humidity.

Are you a licensed tile contractor near me?

Yes. We're a licensed and insured tile contractor serving St. Petersburg, Clearwater, the Gulf Beaches, and the Tampa Bay area, and we can provide documentation before your project starts.
